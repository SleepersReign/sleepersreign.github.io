Sleepers' Reign Official Website

Homepage of band Sleepers' Reign. Sleepers' Reign is a music project that strikes a balance between dreamt up electronic soundscapes and real performances. The group was founded in 2010, when Lukas Hermans, Orson Wouters and Ruben Mertens joined forces, recording songs in their bedroom studio in Herentals, Belgium.
